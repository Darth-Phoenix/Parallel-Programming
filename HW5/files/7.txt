After more than 40 years, Formula 1 will return to Las Vegas, USA.
Las Vegas is becoming even more popular, and many tourists visit for more than a few days. The city hosts many big sports events, and among the sports are soccer, ice hockey, and basketball. These events attract more people to the city.
Formula 1 is one of the most popular sports in the United States right now, and Las Vegas is a great place for the event. The city has 150,000 hotel rooms ready for the visitors. The race will happen next year, and it will be one of the most important events for the teams.
